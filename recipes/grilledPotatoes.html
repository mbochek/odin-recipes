Grilled Potatoes

Description

These crispy grilled potatoes are tossed in the best potato seasoning and cooked in foil. Enjoy perfectly crisp potatoes without heating up your kitchen!

Ingredients for seasoning.

  • 1 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon Rosemary
  • ¾ teaspoons Oregano
  • ¾ teaspoons Thyme
  • ¾ teaspoons Sage
  • 1/8 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix all items for seasoning.
  2. Dice potatoes to desired size
  3. Lightly douse potatoes in olive oil.
  4. Cover potatoes in seasoning.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes at 350 degrees, check to see if done.
  6. Enjoy!
